About


   Sai Kala Shreshte - a place of Fine Arts since 2012 stands as an establishing stage for many youngest by dr.Nandhini prakash

  kala_shreshte "Am always and will be profound to enlighten minds and excel individuals with grandeur personification of Art, most probably the finest way of communication to craft the skill for the best portrayal of inner self" Nandhini Prakash Director, Kala Shreshte 20 years of expertise in Carnatic Vocal & Bharathanatyam Since 1998,

   Smt. Nandhini Prakash avidly serving as Guru in Carnatic Music and Bharathanatiyam still continues to hit the passionate learners and since 2012 through her own institution." KALA SHRESHTE an established institution since 2012 by Smt. Nandhini Prakash, a well known disciple of Guru Smt. Lavanya Shankar, herself a disciple of Kalaimamani D.J.Sarasa.

  The centre offers courses in long term and short term durations for Carnatic music, Veena, Keyboard, Drawing, Violin, Guitar, Bharathanatiyam, Zumba and Yoga. We have achieved, Five salangai poojais with 17 students, including a girl aged just 7 and rendered two mesmerising carnatic music festivals. In 2017, MAYURA Our students have excelled by contributing their NATIVAN talent in Mayura and Marudha Natyanjali programme in Thiruvidaimaruthur and Mayiladudhurai, Perur Natiyanjali in Coimbatore.
